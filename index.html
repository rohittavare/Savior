We believe that community safetly can only manifest itself if the community works together. That is why we created Savior, a way for people to build a safer community. Savior is an app that lets you travel safely at night, or any time of the day. Summoning a savior calls a trained and certified walking companion to your location. Our app and map gives travel times for your nearest savior. In pressing situations, pressing the circular alert button broadcasts an alert to nearby saviors. Our android application also collects video and audio footage of the incident to record what happens. Anyone with a First Aid certification can volunteer their time to make communities safer, one savior at a time.
